Pancake

Beat egg until fluffy. Add milk and melted margarine. Add dry ingredients and mix well. Heat a heavy griddle or fry pan which is greased with a little butter on a paper towel. The pan is hot enough when a drop of water breaks into several smaller balls which 'dance' around the pan. Pour a small amount of batter (approx 1/4 cup) into pan and tip to spread out or spread with spoon. When bubbles appear on surface and begin to break, turn over and cook the other side.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • table spond butter
  • teaspoon baking power
  • teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat until creamy
  2. Melt the butter in a microwave-safe bowl
  3. Add the butter and milk to the mix
  4. Heat the frying pan to a medium low flame
  5. Sprinkle a few flecks of water onto your pan
  6. Pour about 3 tablespoons to 1/4 cup batter from the tip of a large spoon or from a pitcher onto the hot griddle or greased frying pan.
  7. Cook for about two minutes or until the pancake is golden
  8. Cook the other side until golden and remove
  9. Enjoy!
